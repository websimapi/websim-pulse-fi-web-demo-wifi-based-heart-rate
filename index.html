How it works (brief)
  1. Detrend and normalize the incoming WiFi magnitude stream.
  2. Apply a biquad band‑pass (0.7–3.0 Hz) to isolate plausible heart activity.
  3. Compute autocorrelation over lags mapping to the HR band and pick the dominant period.
  4. Convert the peak lag to bpm and report a confidence from peak sharpness.
